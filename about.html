About

O'Shea's work offers a profound exploration of identity and raw emotion, expressed through a dynamic fusion of abstract expressionism and figurative art.

Her portraiture transcends the physical form, delving into the psychological essence of her subjects to create pieces that resonate with universal human experiences.

Currently, her art is on display at the historic Providence Art Club, with an anticipated upcoming feature at the esteemed Wickford Art Festival.
